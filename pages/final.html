Final Project: African Raw Food Products Website

This project will guide you through adding interactivity to simple website for an African Raw Food Store using JavaScript.

Learning Objectives:

  • Create and use functions to perform tasks.
  • Use prebuilt function such as alert(), prompt(), etc.
  • Use selectors to select and manipulate the DOM of at least 3 of the webpages in your website.
  • Handle one of such events such as click events, mouse events, keyboard events.
  • Showcase your creativity and personalize the website.
